Our Story

Baked with Passion & Events

Baked with Passion & Events is a Lusaka-based luxury events and catering brand dedicated to creating elegant, unforgettable experiences. What started as a passion for baking has evolved into a full-scale event management and catering service that sets the standard for class and creativity in Zambia.

Our founder's vision was simple: to bring high-end, emotional aesthetics to every celebration. From the intricate details of a Chilanga Mulilo booth to the exquisite flavors of our catering menus, we ensure that every event is a masterpiece.
